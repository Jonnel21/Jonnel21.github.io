FAQS

  1. Can I bring a guest?
    • We appreciate that you would love to celebrate our happiness with an extra someone. However, we would like to stick to our current guest list.
  2. What should I wear?
    • It will be pastel themed wedding. However, if you don't have any pastel themed colors, please wear what you have.
  3. Why is there a 1 hour and 30 minute gap?
    • We are going to take our wedding pictures. In the meantime, the venue will be serving cocktails & Hors d'oeuvres, so please enjoy.
  4. When is the RSVP deadline?
    • September 2nd, 2022
  5. What time should I arrive?
    • Ceremony starts at 3PM. If you'd like to attend the ceremony, please arrive around 2.30 - 2.55 PM.
      If you'd like to attend just the reception, please arrive during cocktail hours 4.30 PM - 5.30 PM.
  6. Are kids welcomed?
    • Yes! We would love to receive blessings from the little ones ♥
  7. What kind of food will be served?
    • We will be having a buffet. If you have dietary restrictions, please email ajonnel@gmail.com in advance.
  8. Will it be indoors or outdoors?
    • We will be having an outdoor ceremony, with an indoor reception.
  9. What will the weather be like?
    • It is the beginning of fall, so we would expect a chill weather with hints of warmth around 75-80°F high and 50-55°F low.
  10. Is it okay to take pictures during the wedding?
    • Yes! Feel free to share our happiness with the world. Using our hashtags #LetJOheartBeYORguidingKey.
      However, please be mindful of our photographers during the event.
  11. Will there be an open bar?
    • There will be a cash bar all throughout the event. We do have signature drinks, wine, and champagne served during the reception.
  12. Can I bring my gift to the wedding?
    • Yes, there will be a gift table & card box.
